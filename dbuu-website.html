Scholarships & Financial Aid

Dev Bhoomi Uttarakhand University offers various scholarships to meritorious students and those from economically disadvantaged backgrounds:

  • Merit Scholarship: Up to 50% tuition fee waiver for students with 90% and above in qualifying exams
  • Sports Scholarship: Up to 30% fee waiver for students with achievements in national/international sports
  • Uttarakhand Domicile Scholarship: Special 20% fee concession for students from Uttarakhand
  • Defense Personnel Ward Scholarship: 15% fee concession for wards of defense personnel
  • Alumni Scholarship: 10% fee concession for siblings/wards of DBUU alumni
